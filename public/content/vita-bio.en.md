## Rüdiger Schäfer

- born 1952 in Saarland
- 1973–1978 studies in literature, art history, philosophy in Mainz and Aix-en-Provence
- extensive travels to many countries
- worked in various professions (including sawmill worker, innkeeper, interpreter, gardener, driver, timber trader, forestry worker…)
- 2001 training in family and systemic constellations, psychotherapeutic practitioner
- author (catalogue texts, articles in specialist journals)
- since 2006 freelance artist
